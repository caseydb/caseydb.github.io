This is my journey to becoming a developer

Long term goals

My long term goals are to become a product led founder. Specifically, I’d like to be able to do full stack development within my startup, Amplifier. This will allow me to work alongside my developers to solve problems and build our product stronger and faster.

Strengths and limitations while learning

I have strength with my patience and positive attitude. I really do believe people are capable of achieving incredible things if they put their mind to it. Development is no different.


My biggest weakness is that I’m stretched. I still work full time on my startup in addition to EDA. This will make for 80+ hour weeks during bootcamp.

Human skills I want to develop through EDA

I’d like to strengthen my ability to detect when another person is struggling or thriving. This better empowers those around them, such as myself, to help them.

Managing the workload

To successfully manage the workload of EDA I need to honour time blocking. Having clear and scheduled times for development will give me the space to detach from other work and get my sprints done.

Seeking help

Having the right process to solve problems is important. My first approach if I can’t debug it myself, is to ask Google and Stackoverflow. If no luck, I’ll ask the #foundations channel. As a last resort I will contact my facilitator directly.
