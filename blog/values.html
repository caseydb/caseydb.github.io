This is my journey to becoming a developer


Values

Ethical Decision

In 2018 I left my former job to start a digital marketing agency. By chance, a friend of mine was doing the same thing in Sydney, so I flew over and we teamed up. We were 2 years into a friendship and very excited about the potential.


In the first week I learnt that he had been shoplifting from local markets. To make it worse, he had done it for a rush. Once confirmed, I made the decision to part ways with him because it didn’t align with my values. I have no regrets as our integrity is everything.

Culture and Identity

I grew up in Dunedin and New Plymouth. I whakapapa to Scotland, England, and Norway. I’ve built my identity around impact ventures. I truly believe startups can solve many of the world issues we face.

Strengths and Limitations

My biggest strength is my optimism and positive attitude mixed with a deep caring for others. This has led my life's work to centre around bold ideas to help make the world a better place.


My limitation is that with bold ideas, comes a big workload. It can be a real challenge balancing multiple commitments. I find time blocking works wonders for helping with this.
